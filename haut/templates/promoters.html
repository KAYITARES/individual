Professor Nshuti Manasseh

B.Com. hons. MuK, MBAcc. Abdn, UK, PhD Finance, Abdn, UK.

Chairman Board of Promoters.

The founding of University of Kigali is a dream come true for me to pass on over 23 years of University lecturing and Administrative experience gained from high standards universities of:

  • Strathmore University Nairobi, (a total of 14 years of experience).

  • Catholic University of East-Africa, Nairobi, Kenya (where he was first a Dean of Faculty of Commerce and later after his PhD, a Deputy Vice Chancellor Academic.. a total of 7 years) and Finally;

  • Aberdeen University, UK ( 2 years).

  • Professor Nshuti was Minister of Commerce, Industry, Cooperatives and Tourism, a Minister of Finance and Finally, a Minister of Public Service and Labour of Republic of Rwanda.

  • Professor Nshuti Manasseh is a Professor of Finance. He holds a PhD in Finance from Aberdeen University, UK, a Masters of Business Administration in Accounting, Aberdeen University, UK, and A Bachelor of Commerce (Hons.) from Makerere University.

  • Professor Nshuti Manasseh, was The Chairman Board of Promoters assigned to set up Umutara Polytechnic (University) in Eastern Province, Nyagatare District.

  • He also served as The Chairman Board of Governors assigned to turn round SFB (School of Finance and Banking, Kigali, Rwanda) and after turning it round alongside the then management team, in 2011 decided to start a private University with fellow promoters (University of Kigali) which offers premier/quality University education.

  • We have designed a first class University, that will offer highest quality academic programmes designed to empower our young graduates with rare skills and thus capacity necessary for the job market local and international.

  • We blend our degree programmes with professional programmes such CPA (Certified Public Accountant Course), ACCA (Association of Chartered Certified Accountants), CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst), CISA (Certified Information Systems Auditor) and CISM (Certified Information Security Manager) which reinforces the degrees we offer to international standards.

  • We promise our country men and women as well as our international students and partners, unequalled holistic education, of highest excellence anywhere, and at competitive cost.

  • We promoters/founder members, shall always be defined by uncompromising high quality education, discipline, and integrity befitting our graduates. This is not only a promise, but passionate commitment to you, our esteemed students, to your parents or guardians.

We Labour for The Future.
